Caelesti

A heavily Optifine reliant Vanilla+ pack

Download [Java]


By Skaliber

Caelesti is an advanced vanilla+ pack which takes advantage of many hidden features that are both vanilla, and Optifine reliant to completely change the way that Minecraft is experienced. The pack has a huge focus on custom flora, and fauna, and attempts to improve the many inconsistencies and flaws of the original look of Minecraft.
